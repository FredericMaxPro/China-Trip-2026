Essential China tips
Practical info for European travellers in 2025
📱Apps & internet
  • Install VPN before entering China — Google, Instagram, WhatsApp are blocked
  • WeChat: messaging, maps, payments, QR codes — essential
  • Alipay works for foreigners with foreign card — set up at home
  • Offline maps: Maps.me or Baidu Maps
  • Google Translate offline Chinese pack: download before you go
💳Money & payments
  • Nearly cashless — WeChat Pay or Alipay accepted almost everywhere
  • Carry some Yuan cash for rural areas and markets
  • ATMs in cities accept Visa/Mastercard
  • 1 EUR ≈ 7.8 CNY — always negotiate at markets
  • Tipping is not expected in China
🚆Getting around
  • High-speed trains (G/D trains) excellent between major cities
  • Book on Trip.com — no VPN needed
  • Didi is the Chinese Uber — works with foreign cards
  • Domestic flights cheap on Trip.com
  • Zhangjiajie & Yangshuo best reached by flight + local bus
🍜Food & drink
  • Tap water not drinkable — always bottled or boiled
  • Street food is safe and fantastic — look for busy stalls
  • Vegetarian: say "wǒ chī sù" (我吃素)
  • Sichuan food genuinely spicy — order medium if sensitive
  • Supermarkets great for snacks and toiletries
🏥Health & safety
  • Shangri-La altitude ~3200m — take it slow the first day
  • Air quality varies — Shanghai/Beijing can have poor AQI days
  • Travel insurance with medical cover essential
  • Emergency: 110 (police), 120 (ambulance)
  • Pharmacies (药店) everywhere and well-stocked
🗣Culture & language
  • Basics: xièxie (谢谢) = thank you, nǐ hǎo = hello
  • Mandarin standard — locals appreciate any effort
  • Bargaining at markets: start at 30–40% of asking price
  • Temples: cover shoulders and knees
  • Photographing people: ask first ("kě yǐ pāi zhào ma?")
📍Practical bits
  • Public toilets everywhere — bring tissues & sanitizer
  • Squat toilets common outside tourist areas
  • China SIM: buy at airport on arrival (show passport)
  • Hotel check-in: passport always required by law
  • Book Great Wall & Forbidden City in advance online
🌤Weather late Aug–Sep
  • Shanghai: hot & humid 28–34°C, some rain
  • Yangshuo / Guilin: warm 26–30°C, afternoon showers possible
  • Zhangjiajie: 22–28°C, often misty (great atmosphere)
  • Lijiang: pleasant 18–24°C, cooler at night
  • Shangri-La: cool 12–18°C — pack a warm layer
  • Beijing: warm & clear 22–28°C, perfect autumn weather
